BARINV Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

BARINV Pro ("the App") is an inventory management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an admin account.
  • Business Data: Inventory items, staff names, venue details, purchase orders, guest information, and other business data you enter into the App.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens for delivering real-time alerts.
  • Camera & Photos: Used only when you scan barcodes or upload item/receipt images. We do not access your camera or photos without your permission.
  • Bluetooth: Used only to connect to supported BLE scales for inventory weighing. No other Bluetooth data is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the inventory management service.
  • To sync your data across devices in real time.
  • To send push notifications about stock requests and updates.
  • To improve the App's functionality and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Each business account's data is isolated and protected by row-level security policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or business data with third parties. Your data is yours.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): For delivering push notifications.

6. Your Rights

  • You can export all your data at any time using the CSV export features.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • You can revoke camera, Bluetooth, or notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above.
